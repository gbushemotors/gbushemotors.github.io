Wheel Alignment

Wheel alignment ensures that all four wheels are set correctly. Correct alignment increases tyre life & fuel economy and many more benefits. Wheel alignment can be affected by speed bumps, kerbs and or by wear in suspension components. We recommend getting your wheel alignment checked after fitting new tyres or suspension components. Our team, equipped with a brand new alignment machine, will get your car back to manufacturers specifications.

Adblue

AdBlue is a liquid solution used to treat exhaust gases on modern diesel engines. Its introduced into the exhaust, treatment of diesel exhaust gases in this way allows up to 90% of the produced nitrogen oxides (NOx) by the vehicle to be converted into environmentally friendly components: nitrogen and water. The AdBlue tank needs to be refilled between 10 to 30 thousand kms. If you are presented with an AdBlue level warning, don't ignore this. If your AdBlue tank runs empy, your vehicle will not start. Call in and we will refill it.
