Wheel Alignment

Wheel alignment ensures that the angle of travel of all four wheels are set correctly. Correct alignment increases tyre life & fuel economy, improves handling & driving safety. Wheel alignment can be affected over time by speed bumps, hitting kerbs and or by excessive wear in suspension components. We highly recommend getting your wheel alignment checked after fitting new tyres or suspension components. Our team, equipped with a brand new wheel alignment machine, will get your car back to manufacturers specifications.
